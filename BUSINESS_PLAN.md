# BUSINESS PLAN - Gather

## Vision
Build a community-first social network that can later expand into short-video creation and discovery.

## Phase 1 (MVP): Free Growth Product
- Free app for all users.
- Prioritize:
  - user growth
  - retention
  - community participation
- KPIs:
  - DAU/WAU
  - D30 retention
  - posts per active user
  - comments per post

## Phase 2: Premium Accounts
- Offer premium subscription features:
  - profile customization
  - verified badge
  - advanced creator/community analytics
- Objective: introduce predictable recurring revenue.

## Phase 3: Boosted Posts
- Paid promotion for creators, communities, and local businesses.
- Self-serve boosting controls and performance metrics.

## Phase 4: Creator Monetization
- Creator subscriptions.
- Tips/donations.
- Platform commission: **5%–15%**.

## Phase 5: Ads (Only After Strong Activity)
- Introduce ads only after strong retention and healthy engagement.
- Ads should be additive, not disruptive.

## Product Roadmap (Post-MVP)
After validating community product-market fit:
1. Short video uploads
2. Vertical video feed
3. Creator tools
4. Trending video feed
5. Recommendation system
6. Video monetization

## Technical Strategy
- Maintain modular content architecture where posts can attach multiple media types.
- Keep feed service media-agnostic for easy video insertion.
- Add video processing/transcoding as an isolated service later.
