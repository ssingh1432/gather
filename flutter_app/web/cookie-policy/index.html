This page explains the cookies and similar technologies (like browser local storage and mobile device identifiers) that Gather uses, and your choices for each. It supplements our Privacy Policy.

Managing advertising identifiers on your device

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization" (or "Delete advertising ID" on newer Android versions).
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, or Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → turn off "Personalized Ads."

Opting out reduces ad personalization but you may still see ads.

Browser storage on the web app

The Gather web app primarily uses browser local storage rather than traditional tracking cookies to keep you signed in. You can clear this through your browser's settings, but doing so will sign you out.
